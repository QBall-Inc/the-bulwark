## Learnings

- Sub-agents follow ALL applicable rules, not just the most specific ones. When generic rules (SA2) and specific agent output specs both apply, agents will produce outputs for both. Rules must be explicit about precedence.
- Extra `.md` log files from sub-agents are a symptom of ambiguous output instructions, not a hook or backgrounding issue.
