## Learnings

1. **Backticks in SKILL.md**: Avoid backticks containing bash-special characters (`!`, `$`, `()`) - they get interpreted during skill loading
2. **Skill descriptions**: Must be single-line with "Use when" triggers for auto-loading
3. **Mandatory steps**: Place at start of workflow or as numbered phase, not just at end of document
4. **Completion checklists**: Add explicit checklist for mandatory steps to prevent omission
